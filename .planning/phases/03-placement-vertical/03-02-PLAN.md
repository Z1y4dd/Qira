<objective>
Seed the placeholder Arabic placement bank into Supabase: 5 staggered-difficulty passages (Levels 2/6/10/14/18), 15 multiple-choice questions (3 per passage: literal/vocabulary/inferential per D-05), 60 choices (4 per question). All rows tagged `is_placeholder = true` so a future literacy-specialist swap is a single DELETE-WHERE-placeholder + INSERT-real SQL transaction. All Arabic text is NFC-normalized inline (same pattern as `src/db/seed/index.ts`). The seed is idempotent (uses `placement_bank_version` to skip if already seeded at version 1).

Purpose: PLAC-02 (hand-authored bank of ~15 MCQ items spanning 4–6 staggered-difficulty passages) and PLAC-08 (placement results persisted with `kind = 'placement'` — which requires the bank to exist before any attempt can be recorded). The Arabic content drafted here is **a smoke-test prop, not a pedagogically-calibrated instrument** — final content is the literacy specialist's deliverable (Phase 5 gate).

Output: 5 texts, 15 questions, 60 choices in the live DB with `is_placeholder = true`; `pnpm db:seed:placement` is idempotent; `pnpm test:run src/db/seed/placement-placeholder.test.ts` exits 0.
</objective>
