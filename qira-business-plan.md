2. The Problem
Arabic literacy is in crisis. Research consistently shows that a significant percentage of Arabic-speaking students in grades 1–6 cannot read at their grade level. Yet the tools available to parents and teachers remain outdated, generic, or simply non-existent.
Key Pain Points
•	No leveling system: No standardized Arabic reading level framework exists for children (unlike Guided Reading Levels or Lexile in English)
•	Lack of differentiated content: Textbook content is rigid; teachers have no access to differentiated reading materials
•	Apps miss the mark: Most Arabic learning apps focus on alphabet and vocab — not reading comprehension or fluency
•	Parent blindspot: Parents have no easy way to know their child's reading level or track progress at home
•	Diglossia challenge: Arabic has diglossia (Fusha vs. Ammiyya), creating unique complexity that global platforms cannot address
Validated by Teacher Research
Our preliminary teacher survey (n=47) revealed that over 70% of teachers report more than half their students read below grade level, and 85% say there is no clear system for measuring reading levels at their school.

3. Our Solution — Qira Platform
Qira is a web and mobile platform that delivers three core capabilities in Arabic:
Core Features (MVP)
•	📊  Reading Assessment Engine: AI-driven placement quiz that assigns a reading level (Levels 1–20, mapped to grades 1–6)
•	📚  Leveled Content Library: Curated Arabic short stories, non-fiction texts, and poems at each level
•	❓  Adaptive Comprehension Questions: Auto-generated comprehension, vocabulary, and inference questions after each text
•	📈  Progress Tracking Dashboard: Parent/teacher dashboard showing reading level, progress over time, and weak areas
•	🏆  Gamification & Motivation System: Stars, badges, and streak system to keep children engaged daily
Phase 2 Features (Post-MVP)
•	🎙️  Read-Aloud with AI: AI tutor that reads aloud with the child and corrects pronunciation in real-time
•	🏫  School Management Portal: School and classroom management tools, reports, and bulk licensing
•	🌍  Multilingual Expansion: English, French, Urdu — expanding to serve multilingual families
